About

Talaqqi is an innovative online platform designed to make Qur’an learning accessible, authentic, and interactive for students everywhere. By combining the traditional teacher-student method of guided recitation with modern technology, the platform allows learners to receive live instruction while also benefiting from AI-powered feedback on their recitation. With Talaqqi, students can join real-time classes, view a synchronized digital mushaf, annotate verses, and track their progress — all in one cohesive system. The platform ensures that learners experience the depth and authenticity of classical Qur’an education while overcoming geographical and technological barriers. Talaqqi is perfect for anyone seeking a personalized, reliable, and spiritually enriching learning experience, whether at home, in a mosque, or remotely. It bridges tradition and technology to create an intuitive, interactive, and effective Qur’an learning environment for the modern world.

Features Overview

Talaqqi is designed to deliver an interactive, authentic, and personalized Qur’an learning experience. Its key features include: User-Friendly Accounts & Security: Create accounts easily, securely log in, and manage profiles. Verified teachers ensure a trusted learning environment. Live Interactive Classes: Teachers can schedule sessions, and students can join real-time classes with video and audio streaming. Synchronized Digital Mushaf: Students and teachers can view the Qur’an together in real time, with dynamic highlighting for smooth follow-along. Annotation & Notes: Teachers can annotate verses, add notes, and edit them; students can view these annotations to enhance understanding. AI-Powered Recitation Feedback: Students can record their recitations, receive AI-driven Tajweed analysis, and get instant suggestions for improvement. Progress Tracking & Recommendations: Track learning progress, monitor mastery of recitation rules, and receive personalized practice recommendations based on AI insights. Multi-User & Role Management: The system supports multiple participants per session, with distinct roles for students, teachers, and administrators. Talaqqi integrates tradition with technology to provide an all-in-one platform for learners seeking a structured, reliable, and spiritually enriching Qur’an education — accessible anytime, anywhere.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to make authentic Qur’an learning accessible to every student, regardless of distance or circumstances. Talaqqi brings the classical teacher-student tradition into the digital age, offering a guided learning experience that is accurate, interactive, and spiritually uplifting. By combining human wisdom with intelligent technology, our goal is to support learners at every stage—helping them improve their recitation, deepen their understanding, and stay connected to qualified teachers around the world.
